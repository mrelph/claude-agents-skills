## ABSENCE CONTINGENCY PLANS

At 18U level, player absences are common. Here's how to handle them:

### If Marsh (1.00) is Out:
- Move Relph to his spot
- Promote Freeman or Silliker to top line
- Adjust special teams around Relph

### If Relph (1.00) is Out:
- Move Marsh to his spot  
- Promote Freeman to top line
- Adjust special teams around Marsh

### If Both Elite Players Out (crisis):
- Freeman, Klakring, Silliker become top line
- McCredy moves up to second line
- Play defensive, structured game
- Lower expectations, focus on effort

### If Forwards Are Missing:
- Move F/D players (Marsh, Relph, Freeman, Berry, Tegart, Silliker) to forward
- This is easy - we have options

### If Defensemen Are Missing:
- Move F/D players (Marsh, Relph, Freeman, Berry, Tegart, Silliker) to defense
- **This is our superpower** - we have 9 D-capable players
- Can handle multiple D absences easily

### If Goalies Are Missing:
- Haffey can play forward (emergency only)
- Schuchart can play defense (emergency only)
- Try to borrow goalie from another team if possible
