# Report Templates

Standard templates for consolidated research reports.

---

## Template 1: Executive Research Report

Best for: Decision-makers who need key findings quickly with supporting detail available.

```markdown
# [Research Topic] - Consolidated Research Report

**Date:** [Date]
**Sources Analyzed:** [Count]
**Overall Confidence:** [High/Medium/Low]

---

## Executive Summary

### Key Findings
1. **[Finding 1 Title]** (Confidence: HIGH)
   [One-sentence summary]

2. **[Finding 2 Title]** (Confidence: MEDIUM)
   [One-sentence summary]

3. **[Finding 3 Title]** (Confidence: HIGH)
   [One-sentence summary]

### Critical Insights
- [Most important insight]
- [Second most important]
- [Third most important]

### Areas of Uncertainty
- [Key conflict or gap #1]
- [Key conflict or gap #2]

### Recommended Actions
1. [Top recommendation]
2. [Second recommendation]
3. [Third recommendation]

---

## Methodology

### Sources Analyzed

| ID | Source Type | Name | Date | Credibility |
|----|-------------|------|------|-------------|
| S1 | AI Model | Claude Deep Research | [Date] | High |
| S2 | AI Model | GPT-4 Analysis | [Date] | High |
| S3 | Document | [Report Name] | [Date] | High |
| S4 | Web Research | [Source] | [Date] | Medium |

### Consolidation Approach
[Brief description of how sources were analyzed and synthesized]

### Confidence Scoring
Confidence levels reflect:
- Source agreement (40%)
- Evidence quality (30%)
- Source authority (20%)
- Recency (10%)

---

## Detailed Findings

### Finding 1: [Title]

**Confidence Level:** HIGH (0.87)

**Consolidated Position:**
[Detailed statement of the finding, 2-3 sentences]

**Source Perspectives:**

| Source | Position | Evidence Provided |
|--------|----------|-------------------|
| S1 (Claude) | [Summary] | [Key evidence] |
| S2 (GPT-4) | [Summary] | [Key evidence] |
| S3 (Report) | [Summary] | [Key evidence] |

**Analysis:**
[Your synthesis explaining why this finding is reliable]

**Caveats:**
- [Limitation 1]
- [Limitation 2]

---

### Finding 2: [Title]

**Confidence Level:** MEDIUM (0.65)

[Same structure as Finding 1]

---

### Finding 3: [Title]

[Same structure]

---

## Areas of Conflict

### Conflict 1: [Topic]

**Nature of Disagreement:**
[Explain what sources disagree about]

**Position A:**
- Sources: S1, S3
- Claim: [Their position]
- Evidence: [Their support]

**Position B:**
- Sources: S2
- Claim: [Their position]
- Evidence: [Their support]

**Resolution:**
[How you resolved this OR why it remains unresolved]

**Impact on Findings:**
[How this affects your conclusions]

---

## Research Gaps

| Gap | Description | Impact | Recommendation |
|-----|-------------|--------|----------------|
| [Topic] | Not covered by sources | [Significance] | [Suggested action] |
| [Topic] | Only one source | [Significance] | [Suggested action] |
| [Topic] | Data may be outdated | [Significance] | [Suggested action] |

---

## Recommendations

### Immediate Actions
1. **[Action]** - [Rationale based on findings]
2. **[Action]** - [Rationale]

### Further Research Needed
1. [Topic requiring more investigation]
2. [Topic requiring more investigation]

### Risk Mitigation
1. [Risk from findings] → [Mitigation suggestion]

---

## Appendices

### Appendix A: Source Details
[Full information about each source]

### Appendix B: Claim Alignment Matrix
[Complete matrix showing how claims align across sources]

### Appendix C: Confidence Calculations
[Detailed confidence scoring for each finding]

### Appendix D: Raw Extractions
[Original extracted claims from each source]

---

*Report generated by Research Consolidator Skill*
*[Date] | [Version]*
```

## Template 3: Findings Summary (Brief)

Best for: Quick overview when detail is available elsewhere.

```markdown
# [Research Topic] - Findings Summary

**Date:** [Date] | **Sources:** [Count] | **Overall Confidence:** [Level]

---

## Top 5 Findings

### 1. [Finding Title] ⬆️ HIGH CONFIDENCE
[Two-sentence summary of finding]
*Sources: S1, S2, S4 agree*

### 2. [Finding Title] ⬆️ HIGH CONFIDENCE
[Two-sentence summary]
*Sources: All sources agree*

### 3. [Finding Title] ➡️ MEDIUM CONFIDENCE
[Two-sentence summary]
*Sources: S1, S3 agree; S2 differs slightly*

### 4. [Finding Title] ➡️ MEDIUM CONFIDENCE
[Two-sentence summary]
*Sources: S2 only (not addressed by others)*

### 5. [Finding Title] ⬇️ LOW CONFIDENCE
[Two-sentence summary]
*Sources: S1, S3 conflict; S2 neutral*

---

## Conflicts Noted

| Topic | Conflict | Resolution |
|-------|----------|------------|
| [Topic] | [Brief description] | [How resolved] |

---

## Research Gaps

- [ ] [Topic not covered]
- [ ] [Topic needs more depth]

---

## Recommended Next Steps

1. [Action item]
2. [Action item]
3. [Action item]

---

*Summary generated [Date]*
```

## Template Selection Guide

| Use Case | Recommended Template |
|----------|---------------------|
| C-suite briefing | Executive Report |
| Detailed analysis review | Executive Report |
| Comparing AI model outputs | Comparison Matrix |
| Quick stakeholder update | Findings Summary |
| API/tool integration | Data Export (JSON) |
| Audit/compliance | Executive Report + Appendices |
| Follow-up research planning | Findings Summary + Gaps focus |

---

## Customization Notes

### Adding Custom Sections

You can add sections for:
- Risk assessment
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Timeline implications
- Stakeholder impact
- Implementation considerations

### Adjusting Detail Level

**More Detail:**
- Expand source perspectives in findings
- Add raw claim extractions
- Include full confidence calculations

**Less Detail:**
- Use Findings Summary template
- Reduce appendices
- Focus on top 3-5 findings only

### Branding/Formatting

Templates can be customized with:
- Organization logo/header
- Custom color scheme
- Specific terminology
- Regulatory compliance statements
